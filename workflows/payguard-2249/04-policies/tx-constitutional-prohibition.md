Texas constitutionally prohibits wage garnishment for consumer debts per CPRC 63.004: current wages for personal service are not subject to garnishment. Only child support, alimony, federal tax levies, and federal student loans are permitted. If order_type is CONSUMER_CREDITOR, status = PROHIBITED, withholding = 0.
